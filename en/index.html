Frequently asked questions

We answer your questions.

Which service level suits me?+
It depends on how much you want to delegate. Relación gives you continuous support; Implicación and Compromiso add maintenance and full system management; Apoyo covers one-off projects such as implementations or training. We help you choose at the first meeting, with no commitment.
Do you only work in Madrid?+
Our office is on Paseo de la Castellana, but the team is always available digitally with integrated tools, so we support clients across Spain. You can also book a meeting to be seen in person.
Do you only do certification?+
No. We don't want companies to stop at the certificate: we help them work in an agile, simple way towards their strategic goals. The certificate is a consequence of managing well.
What is Consultify and how is it different?+
Consultify is our digital line: subscription-based ISO consulting with fixed pricing and an online calculator, powered by AI. TuConsultor is the tailored consulting firm; Consultify is the product for those who prefer self-service with expert support.

Purpose
Our purpose
The Instituto de Excelencia Europea was created with the purpose of being a meeting point for the different agents interested in strengthening and consolidating excellence as the foundation of organisational management and personal development. It specialises in management excellence, EFQM Model training, innovation models, EFQM assessments, EFQM seals and advanced management and good-practice routes.
∞
Vision
To be a reference for organisations and people committed to excellence and to sharing knowledge, experience and good management practice.

Our purpose
TRESCORE PROYECTOS ITE, S.L. is the group's parent company and operates under the TuConsultor brand. Its purpose is to transform the concept of consulting through a client-relationship model that enables Consulting as a Service: the Digital, Human, Cutting-edge Ecosystem. A team of consultants always available digitally with integrated tools, structured in three dimensions — documentation, technology and talent — and four service levels: Relación, Implicación, Compromiso and Apoyo.
